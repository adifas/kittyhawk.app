Rocket Launch Program

A progressive journey to Low Earth Orbit through certified rocketry development and launches.

Progressive Certification

Start with Level 1 certification (H and I impulse motors) and progress through Level 2 and 3 for larger payloads.

18-Month Journey

Structured program starting with F-class motors and culminating in an O-class rocket launch to Low Earth Orbit.

Safety First

Comprehensive safety protocols and certification requirements at each stage of development.

Certification Levels

Level 1

Certification for H and I impulse class motors (solid and hybrid). Some F and G motors may require this level.

Level 2

Advanced certification for J, K, and L impulse class motors, enabling larger payloads and higher altitudes.

Level 3

Expert certification for M, N, and O impulse class motors, required for Low Earth Orbit missions.

Program Timeline

Initial Phase

  • Months 1-6:

    Level 1 certification and F/G class motor launches

  • Months 7-12:

    Level 2 certification and H-K class motor launches

Advanced Phase

  • Months 13-15:

    Level 3 certification and L-M class motor launches

  • Months 16-18:

    O-class rocket development and Low Earth Orbit launch
